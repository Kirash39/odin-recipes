Teriyaki Meatballs

Description

Almost your typical meatballs, but with a twist! We're using teriyaki to make them even better.

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons garlic, minced
  • 2 large white eggs

Sauce

  • 1 (15 ounce) can crushed pinapple
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 pinch garlic salt, or to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup cold water
