Blueberry oatmeal bars

Description

If you wanna have a sweet, tasty and healthy snack, this is the recipe for you!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole oats
  • 1 cup oat flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/3 cup shredded coconut (Optional)
